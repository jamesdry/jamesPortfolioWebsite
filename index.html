    I pursued my education from kindergarten to highschool at Chandra Kumala School Medan. From there, I have a dream to study abroad in Singapore, so I did. I took IELTS Exam and because of my IELTS results, I succeed to get in to one of the university I wanted in Singapore. The university is called James Cook University. I got my Diploma of Higher Education Majoring in Information Technology title in just 8 months. After I got my diploma, I applied for many jobs in Medan at that time.

I had to get another title because it is hard to find a job in Indonesia with just a Diploma Degree. So here I am now as an Information System student at Universitas Pelita Harapan, Lippo Karawaci, Tangerang, Banten.
